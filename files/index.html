About

Why Choose Us?

Do you know that Human beings spend approximately 6 months of their lives waiting in line for things, it means like 3 days a year of queueing up.It is a great loss of our precious life-time if we look at the bigger picture. Thus, this very idea provided us with the motivation to make this product- "AFSC". Through this, We aim to provide a self sufficient, safe and hassle free experience to the consumer by saving their precious time they waste standing in queues to fill the mundane forms and speed up the process exponentially with the help of our device. That's just a blip on the radar !

  • AFSC provides "On-the-Go" ability for its users with the sustainance of Q-R Codes.
  • Now thinking from the perspective of bank staff, AFSC provides an extremely reliable and catalogued way of all the forms from the people.
  • Making our New system much more managable and organised than previous loosely arranged heaps of forms that no one can get hold of without getting a backache.So, we got you covered.
  • So, Scan it, Fill it and Submit it, all in the same place with minimum contact. Isn't it amazing?

It is just a teaser of the levels of implementation and applications AFSC has in various scenarios sectors of banking and how automating and changing our tradition ways can save the people and banks tremendous aomunts of time, money, resources, work-force and what not.

Problems our idea solves

Benefits the bank

Banking services have always been the source of major inconvenience to the customers mainly due to inefficient working, lousy form handling and deposition for money withdrawal, deposit or transfer. We intend to blend the credibility of QR-code scanning with efficient web-hosting to make the process simpler and faster.

  • A large number of forms are misplaced or lost which causes a lot of problems keeping up with the hard copies.
  • The vision was not only to help the consumers but also to provide a smooth and faster way of filling forms related to money withdrawal, transfer or deposit so that the life of the banke employee can be made a little easier.
  • Lastly, it provides a sense of relief for the employees since all the forms are saved in secure firebase storage which provides high level protection and provides the employee, a much needed sense of relief.

All Forms at one location

Who doesn't like a catalogued of all the things we need at the same place and time at the scan of a Q-R !

  • Whether you are looking to open a bank account or deposit a cheque, we got you covered.
  • You have a choice, to look for that one form you want to use or scan, sit and maybe have a samosa while your form is being conveniently preinted out waiting for you to fill it. What would you do?

    • Its easy, good and fast and makes our lives better. What else can we ask for?

Contactless and Queue less Platform

Contactless and completely digital system that ensures a smooth banking experience coupled with security. Contactless and social Distancing in general is something whose worth we understood in recent times only and value how important they are in general.

  • As we all are aware of how global the world is now-a-days, which can be dangerous in times like right now where COVID-19 pandemics spread its wrath. We may have won battle this time but we should be prepared for the war since we were clearly not ready this time. Hence, AFSC's smooth and almost negligible contact makes it an even more requirement in today's times.
  • Making this system contactless was a major emphasis because we know the process of banking in overwhelmingly long and a single piece of paper ends up on hands of so many people, hence higher chances of spreading unwanted pathogen.
